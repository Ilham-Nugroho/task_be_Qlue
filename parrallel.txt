concurrency is when two (or more) tasks running side by side, 
one task's portion at a time,
example:
task A running to do some of it's portion,
task A then will be paused to start task B's portion,
repeatedly takes turn untill they bot seemingly finished together



parrallel is when two(or more) tasks are running at the same time,
started and finished at the same time too
